Welcome to Raigadkar Agro

 your trusted partner in agricultural solutions and services. Founded in 2012 by the visionary Mr. Prakash Ughade Raigadkar, our company has been dedicated to serving farmers and agricultural enthusiasts with excellence and integrity.

At Raigadkar Agro, We offer a comprehensive range of products and services to support every aspect of agriculture:

  • Chemical and Organic fertilizers
  • High-quality seeds
  • Effective Pesticides
  • Advanced drip and sprinkler systems
  • Reliable PVC and HDPE pipes
  • Innovative terrace gardening solutions

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond just selling products. We believe in empowering farmers
with knowledge and expertise to enhance productivity and sustainability. That's why our team of experienced professionals is always ready to provide personalized consultancy services tailored to your specific needs.
