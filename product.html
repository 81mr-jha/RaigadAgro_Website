Welcome to Raigadkar Agro - Your One-Stop Destination for Premium Agricultural Supplies

At Raigadkar Agro, we understand the dedication and hard work that goes into nurturing crops and ensuring a bountiful harvest. That's why we're committed to providing farmers and agricultural enthusiasts with an unmatched selection of top-quality supplies to support their endeavors. 

With a wide range of products and services tailored to meet the diverse needs of modern farming, we are your trusted partner in agricultural success.

Effective Pesticides

Protect your crops from pests and diseases with our range of effective pesticides. Our carefully curated selection includes both chemical and organic options, so you can choose the solution that best fits your pest management needs while minimizing environmental impact.

Advanced Irrigation Systems

Promote water conservation and efficient irrigation with our state-of-the-art drip and sprinkler systems. Designed to deliver precise water distribution to your crops, our irrigation systems help optimize water usage while ensuring optimal plant hydration and growth.

Terrace Gardening Supplies

Ensure reliable water distribution and drainage on your farm with our high-quality PVC and HDPE pipes. From irrigation networks to drainage systems, our durable piping solutions are built to withstand the rigors of agricultural use, providing long-lasting performance and peace of mind.

Durable Piping Solutions

Embrace urban agriculture and grow fresh produce anywhere with our comprehensive terrace gardening solutions. From containers and soil mixes to tools and accessories, we have everything you need to create a thriving garden on your terrace or balcony.

Reliable Tractor Sales and Services

Count on Raigadkar Agro as your authorized dealer for top tractor brands. Whether you're in the market for a new tractor or need maintenance and repair services for your existing equipment, our experienced team is here to support you every step of the way.

Pound Engraving & Geomembrane Lining

Optimize your pond and water management systems with our specialized services. From pond engraving to geomembrane lining installation, we offer tailored solutions to help you maximize water retention and minimize seepage, ensuring efficient water storage and utilization.

Protect your crops, equipment, and storage areas from the elements with our heavy-duty tarpaulins. Constructed from high-quality materials, our tarpaulins provide durable and reliable protection against sun, rain, wind, and more, helping safeguard your valuable assets year-round.
